How to Accept A Request

Once you have posted a favor, accepting a request and assigning the favor to a user is easy!

Normal Mode

1. Go to your profile and click the tab for your "Favors".

2. Find the favor you are wanting to assign and click the "Assign Favor" button.

3. This will take you to a list of all users who have requested your favor. To assign the favor to a specific user, just click accept! The favor will now be assigned to that user to be completed!

Easy Mode

1. Go to the "Your Favors" tab.

2. Find the favor you are wanting to assign and click the "Assign Favor" button.

3. This will take you to a list of all users who have requested your favor. To assign the favor to a specific user, just click accept! The favor will now be assigned to that user to be completed!
