How to Pay and Review

Once your favor has been completed, you will need to pay the user!

Normal Mode

1. Go to your profile and click the tab for your "Favors".

2. Find the favor you are wanting to assign and click the "Pay And Review" button.

3. Fill out the required information (review, rating, and acknowledge the terms and conditions).

4. This will take you to PayPal's mobile interface. You will need a PayPal account to sign in and to complete the payment. Sign in using your PayPal credentials (not Middleman).

5. Review the payment details and click pay!

Easy Mode

1. Go to the "Your Favors" tab.

2. Find the favor you are wanting to assign and click the "Pay And Review" button.

3. Fill out the required information (review, rating, and acknowledge the terms and conditions).

4. This will take you to PayPal's mobile interface. You will need a PayPal account to sign in and to complete the payment. Sign in using your PayPal credentials (not Middleman).

5. Review the payment details and click pay!
